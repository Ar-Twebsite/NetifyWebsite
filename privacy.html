Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

This policy explains what personal data Netify collects, why, and the rights you have over it. Netify turns your documents into personalized video courses — protecting the information you share with us matters to us.

1. Who we are

Netify ("Netify", "we", "us") is an early-stage venture, currently in pilot, run by the team building it. Netify is not yet incorporated as a company; once it is, we will update this page with our full legal and registration details. Until then, the people behind Netify act as the data controller for the personal data described here.

You can reach us about anything in this policy on WhatsApp at +39 351 876 2841.

2. The data we collect

Information you give us

  • Contact & booking data — when you book a call or message us: your name, work email, company, company size, and anything you choose to tell us.
  • Customer Content — documents, files and materials you share so we can create courses for you.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data — our hosting provider records standard server logs (e.g. IP address, browser type) for security and reliability. We do not run advertising trackers, and we self-host our fonts (no Google Fonts).
  • Scheduling — when you open the booking window, our scheduling provider (Cal.com) may set cookies and process the details you enter to arrange the meeting.

3. Why we use your data and our legal basis

  • To respond to you, run demos and take pre-contract steps — legitimate interest and steps prior to a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b),(f)).
  • To provide our service and create courses — performance of a contract.
  • For Customer Content we act on your instructions as a processor; a data processing agreement governs that use.
  • Where we ask for it, on the basis of your consent (which you can withdraw at any time).

4. Who we share it with

We share data only with service providers who help us operate, under appropriate agreements. These include: scheduling (Cal.com), messaging (WhatsApp / Meta), website hosting (GitHub Pages), and the AI providers we use to generate courses.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your Customer Content to train public or third-party AI models.

5. International transfers

Some providers process data outside the European Economic Area (for example, in the United States). Where they do, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. How long we keep it

We keep contact and booking data for as long as needed to deal with your enquiry and for a reasonable period afterwards. Customer Content is kept for the duration of our engagement and deleted on request or as agreed with you.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you can ask to access, correct, delete, restrict or port your data, and object to certain processing. You can withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us on WhatsApp at +39 351 876 2841. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority — in Italy, the Garante per la protezione dei dati personali.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to keep your information safe.

9. Children

Netify is a business service and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. We'll post any changes on this page and update the date above.
