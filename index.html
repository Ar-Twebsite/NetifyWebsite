FAQ

Questions companies ask.

Straight answers. If yours isn't here, just ask us.

What is Netify?

An AI that turns your documents — or just a goal — into short, personalized video courses. One per person, true to your material.

What do we give it to start?

Your material: policies, manuals, decks, product docs. No files yet? It can start from a goal you describe and you add sources later.

Is the content accurate?

It's built only from your sources — it doesn't invent facts. You can review a course before anything goes out.

How do employees receive their courses?

Delivered where your team already works, each one tailored to that person's role and context — not one video for everyone.

Where does our data go?

Your files stay yours. We use them only to build your courses, never to train public models. EU-based and GDPR-aligned.

Does it fit our existing tools?

Yes. Use Netify on its own, or plug it into your LMS or product through our API.

How fast is it, and what does it cost?

Courses are ready in minutes. Pricing depends on scale — tell us your case and we'll show what you'd save. Quickest start: a 20-minute live demo.
