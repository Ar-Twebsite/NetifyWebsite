FAQ

Questions companies ask.

Straight answers. If yours isn't here, just ask us.

Is the course actually accurate?

Yes. Netify builds from your files and sources — it doesn't invent facts. With no files, it starts from a clear brief you can check before anything is made.

Where does our data go?

Your files stay yours. We use them to build your course, not to train public models. EU-based and GDPR-aligned.

Do we need IT to set this up?

No. You start from a simple chat. For teams, courses arrive where people already work — there's no platform to build and no rollout project.

Can it plug into our LMS or product?

Yes. Netify is an API. Your platform or product can generate courses too, without building the engine yourself.

How fast is a real course?

Minutes, not months. A custom course that used to take about a year can be ready while you're still on the call.

What does it cost?

It depends on scale. Tell us your case and we'll show you what you'd save. The quickest path is a 20-minute call where we make a course live, in front of you.
