Manifesto

The next frontier isn't training AI. It's training people.

Why Netify exists, in plain words. This page comes from our first website, and it is still where we start from.

The problemThe ideaThe principlesYour files

Training is built for everyone. So it fits no one.

One course for all fits no one, and it does not stick. A custom course costs a fortune and takes the better part of a year. And training that ignores a person's actual job is gone by Friday.

Your documents in. A course for each person out.

Give us your material: policies, manuals, decks. Netify prepares short lessons, true to your sources, shaped by the role and context of the person receiving them. Not one video for all: each person gets their part.

People stay in charge.

Videos stay true to the sources. Every version is checked by a person before use. The trainer's method stays recognizable. Time to train people, not AI.

Your files stay yours.

The material you share is used to prepare your courses. Nothing else.
