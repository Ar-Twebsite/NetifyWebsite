The role

03

The situation

Determines whether to respond, ask for help, transfer, or escalate.

What to know before you start

What material do you need?

Playbooks, call scripts, objection banks, escalation rules, and tone guidance.

Who approves the lessons?

Someone who knows the sales or support rules and can confirm that the proposed response is correct.

What does Netify not do?

It does not replace CRM, help-desk, or operating processes. It prepares training that explains how to use those rules.
