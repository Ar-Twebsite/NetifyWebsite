What to know before you start

Which courses work best?

Modules with a clear method but many possible examples, such as feedback, delegation, conflict, one-to-ones, and difficult decisions.

What does the trainer provide?

The model to teach, mistakes to avoid, acceptable examples, and the questions used to judge its application.

What stays out?

Activities that require a trainer to be present, undocumented sensitive cases, and judgments that cannot be checked against the material.
