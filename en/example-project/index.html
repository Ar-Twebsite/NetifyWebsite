How it works

A feedback module for newly appointed managers.

The method is already defined. Netify helps turn it into short lessons and make it recognizable for different roles.

01

Material

Workshop outline, slides, trainer notes, exercises, and examples.

02

Shared lesson

The objective, feedback principle, and stages that must not change.

03

Three versions

New manager, sales manager, and team leader.

04

Videos and quizzes

First versions of the lessons and questions included in the project.

05

Review

The trainer corrects the tone, examples, accuracy, and anything that needs reworking.

What to know before you start

What would you see during a pilot?

The material used, the choices made, the first versions, and the points that need a decision from your team.

Is this a product screenshot?

No. It is an illustrative outline of the work, not a customer result or a finished product interface.

Is the result ready to distribute?

Only after the person responsible for the course has corrected and approved it for the intended use.
