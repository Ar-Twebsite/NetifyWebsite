How it works

You do not have to choose between a generic course and a fully bespoke project. Reuse what works and change only what the client needs.

Trainers and experts correct every version before it is used.

What to know before you start

What material do you need?

A program you already deliver, plus trainer decks, notes, exercises, or other material that captures your method.

Who needs to review it?

Someone who knows the course and can judge its accuracy, tone, and examples.

When is it a poor fit?

When the program is still undefined or nobody can make time to review the first versions.
