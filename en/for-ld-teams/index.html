How it works

The knowledge changes form but remains under your control.

To use Netify, you need a reliable source and someone who can decide whether each version is correct.

01

The source

Reliable procedures, onboarding material, manuals, and operating guidance.

02

The people

Different roles, contexts, and starting experience.

03

The approver

A person or team responsible for the accuracy of the content.

What to know before you start

Which cases work best?

Onboarding, role changes, and operating updates based on well-documented procedures or internal knowledge.

Who needs to be involved?

The subject expert and the person who can approve the content for its intended use.

When is it a poor fit?

When the source is uncertain, the rules change constantly, or nobody can take responsibility for review.

Next step

Do you have a reliable source and someone who can check it?

Those are the two conditions to confirm before discussing an internal project.
