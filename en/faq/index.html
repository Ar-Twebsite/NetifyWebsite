What is Netify?

Netify starts with your training material and prepares first versions of videos and quizzes for a specific client, role, or person.

What do we need to provide?

A course, slides, manuals, notes, exercises, or access to an expert who can explain what must be taught.

What do we receive?

The first versions agreed for the project, together with the choices made and the points your team needs to check.

Who approves the result?

A trainer, expert, or another person responsible for the course. Netify does not decide on its own that content is ready.

Do we need a finished course?

No. You do need a reliable foundation and someone who can answer questions about the content.

What can be tailored?

The language, examples, situations, questions, and level of detail can change. The method changes only if you decide it should.

Is Netify an LMS?

No. Netify prepares training content. It does not manage enrolment, catalogues, attendance, or course delivery.

Does Netify replace trainers?

No. The trainer provides the method, defines the limits, and reviews every version before use.

How is pricing decided?

It depends on the material, the number and length of videos, the quizzes, the versions required, and the correction rounds.

What happens on the first call?

We look at the case and decide whether it is worth defining a pilot. The call does not automatically start the work.

How should we handle confidential material?

Do not send confidential material before agreeing why it is needed, who can access it, and how it will be handled.

Can Netify work inside our product?

We assess limited technical projects case by case. There is no immediate or self-service API access.
