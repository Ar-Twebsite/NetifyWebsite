What to know before you start

Do you need a finished course?

No, but you need a reliable foundation: useful material, clear notes, or an expert who can explain what must be taught.

What can change between versions?

The language, examples, situations, questions, and level of explanation. The method changes only if you decide it should.

What does your team review?

Whether the lesson is correct, recognizable, suitable for the audience, and ready for its intended use.
