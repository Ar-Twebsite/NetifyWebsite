How it works

The pilot starts when the work is clear.

Before anything is produced, we agree what you provide, who the course is for, what Netify will prepare, and who will review it.

01

A real course, workshop, deck, or set of documents.

03

04

Approve, correct, expand the project, or stop.

What to know before you start

What happens on the first call?

We look at the goal, the available material, and the people receiving the training. The call establishes whether Netify suits the case.

How is the price worked out?

It depends on the quality of the material, the number and length of videos, the quizzes, the versions required, and the correction rounds.

What does the pilot not promise?

The call does not automatically produce a complete course. Work starts after the proposal, price, and responsibilities are accepted.
