How it works

Fewer dead ends between experts and production.

The expert provides the knowledge. Netify prepares a first version. The e-learning team checks it and decides what happens next.

01

The expert

Provides sources, examples, and criteria for judging the content.

02

Netify

Organizes the material and prepares the agreed videos and quizzes.

03

The e-learning team

Corrects, approves, and moves the work into the tools it already uses.

What to know before you start

Does Netify replace the authoring tool?

No. It prepares material and first versions before authoring, final production, or publishing.

Who controls the learning design?

Your team does. Netify helps prepare the work, but the learning decisions and review remain yours.

When is it useful?

When expert material is sound but requires a lot of work to become coherent lessons that are easy to review.
